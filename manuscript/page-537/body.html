I didn’t even have the words with which to think.  My experiences were so many meaningless pictures.  But as I began to add to my knowledge, and to my vocabulary, I saw something more in my experiences than mere pictures.  I retained the pictures and I found their interpretation.  That was when I began to do good work, when I wrote ‘Adventure,’  ‘Joy,’ ‘The Pot,’ ‘The Wine of Life,’ ‘The Jostling Street,’ the ‘Love-cycle,’ and the ‘Sea Lyrics.’  I shall write more like them, and better; but I shall do it in my spare time.  My feet are on the solid earth, now.  Hack-work and income first, masterpieces afterward.
