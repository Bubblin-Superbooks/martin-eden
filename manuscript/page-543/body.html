It strikes me as dull, and stupid, and mercenary, and tricky.  Anyway I am not adapted for it.  I’d never get beyond a clerkship, and how could you and I be happy on the paltry earnings of a clerk?  I want the best of everything in the world for you, and the only time when I won’t want it will be when there is something better.  And I’m going to get it, going to get all of it.  The income of a successful author makes Mr. Butler look cheap.  A ‘best-seller’ will earn anywhere between fifty and a hundred thousand dollars—sometimes more and sometimes less; but, as a rule, pretty close to those figures.”

She remained silent; her disappointment was apparent.
