Ever the heap grew, and though each bill was duplicated a thousand times, he found only one for two dollars and a half, which was what he owed Maria.  That meant that Maria would not press for payment, and he resolved generously that it would be the only one he would pay; so he began searching through the cast-out heap for hers.  He sought it desperately, for ages, and was still searching when the manager of the hotel entered, the fat Dutchman.  His face blazed with wrath, and he shouted in stentorian tones that echoed down the universe, “I shall deduct the cost of those cuffs from your wages!”  The pile of cuffs grew into a mountain, and Martin knew that he was doomed to toil for a thousand years to pay for them.  Well, there was nothing left to do but kill the manager and burn down the laundry.
